Community Guidelines

Library Rules

To ensure a peaceful and productive environment for all our visitors, please follow these guidelines.

🤫
Maintain Silence Please keep noise to a minimum. Phones on silent mode at all times.
🚫
No Food Inside Sections Food and drinks are allowed only in the designated café areas.
🪪
Carry Valid ID A valid ID or library card is required to enter the building.
🧹
Keep Sections Clean Please return books to their shelves and keep your study space tidy.
💼
No Seat Reservation Seats are first-come-first-served. Do not leave personal items to "save" seats.
🎒
No Large Bags Inside Bags larger than A4 size must be deposited at the locker room.
📷
No Photography Photography is not permitted to protect the privacy of other readers.
🚬
No Smoking Smoking and vaping are strictly prohibited in all areas of the library.
